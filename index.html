Project: Ink Skincare Marketing Campaign

This was a project that featured the Ink Skincare brand. The line of skincare was catered towards the tattooed community, highlighting factors of self-confidence with the unique, raunchy branding. However in this campaign, they wanted to expand their "self-love" campaign to those who are not tattooed (or not yet tatooed ;). This featured a full photoshoot, print and digital marketing materials, and an in person marketing campaign.
